Who is Bill Gates?
_____________________

Bill Gates is an Enterpreneur and businessman. He built the world's largest software business known as Microsoft.
In the process of doing so he became one of the richest men in the world.
He is know for going through technological innovation, with a "keen" business strategy and with aggressive business tactics. In just 25 years he managed to built a multibillion-dollar company and made himself the richest man in the world along the way. It is said that he accomplished this, not by inventing new technology, but by taking the existing technology and adapting to a specific market. He also managed to dominate that market using smart business tactics.
